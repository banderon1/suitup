Kelend Mills

Katherine Mills

Brother and Sister Mills are members of the 19th Ward in the Mapleton Utah Stake. Brother Mills currently serves as a member of the stake high council.

Before moving to Mapleton Brother and Sister Mills lived in Spanish Fork where Brother Mills served as a counselor in the Spanish Fork Utah Canyon View Stake presidency, as a bishop, and as a counselor in a bishopric. Brother Mills served as a full-time missionary in the Japan Nagoya Mission from 1985 to 1987. He and Sister Mills and their family served together in Japan as Brother Mills served as president of the Japan Fukuoka Mission from 2003 to 2006.

Brother Mills works for the Missionary Department. He is currently the Director of International MTCs.

Brother Mills was born in Afton, Wyoming to Richard and Betty Burton Mills and was raised in Laketown, Utah in the Bear Lake area. Sister Mills grew up in the Salt Lake Valley. They were married in the Salt Lake Temple on June 1, 1989 and have three children.
